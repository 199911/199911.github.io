About this blog

What is this blog for?

This blog is my software engineer development log, recording my learning and ideas, so I can review my progress from time to time. It is also my archive of knowledge, I will read my own blog when I forget details of something I have learnt before.

There is three kinds of content in my blog. Most of the content fail into type 1, which is “fragmented information”, small knowledge on some random topic. These small pieces of information may be refined to “insight” later.

The second type of content is “Insight”. “Insights” can be book reviews or commantaries of article. It is not only a piece of information, which also have my idea or experience in it. These “insights” are not very useful, but I believe when I connect insights, they will become my own “knowledge”, which I can apply them and bring values.

The third kind of content contains “knowledge” I have mentions. “Knowledge” can be a framework on solving problems, can be a mind map of a specific topic, or article with experienment and demonstration. These “knowledge” may not in good quality, but at least organized.

Blog structure

I divide the content to few categories, to help me organize the content:

  • Diary
    • I put the problem or bug I found here. Also some short and simple comment on article I read.
    • It is very causal, I may put something not related to software engineer
  • Commentaries
    • I put comment of article I read here
    • Most of them are “fragmented information”, some of them will be “insight”
  • Book review
    • May contain “fragmented information”, “insight”
    • For well organized book, I should create another article to jot down the “knowledge”, instead of putting them in book review
  • Code Review
    • Contain “insight” and / or “knowledge” I learnt by reading library or framework
  • Knowledge
    • Organized “insight” on specific topics
    • Currently I don’t have content in this category
